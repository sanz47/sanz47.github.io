About Me

When I was 13, I became captivated by Siri on iOS, which sparked my interest in technology. My curiosity led me to discover that artificial intelligence and machine learning were the key technologies behind it, and I knew I wanted to contribute to this field.

I pursued this dream by enrolling in Computer Science and Engineering at the Islamic University of Technology (IUT), one of Bangladesh's top universities. IUT provided me with the perfect environment to nurture my knowledge and work on various projects.

I've developed a strong passion for Machine Learning and AI, completing various courses and gaining practical experience through Kaggle competitions. I'm excited about the opportunity to learn from and contribute to the field.
